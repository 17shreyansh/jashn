## Design System Applied

- Primary: `#6B46C1` (Purple)
- Luxury: `#FFD700` / `#D4AF37` (Gold)
- Black: `#1a1a1a` / `#0a0a0a`

- Headings: `Playfair Display` (serif)

- Buttons: Pill-shaped, gold gradient

- Hero: Dark with decorative blurs
- Sections: Alternating light/dark
- Grids: 3-4 columns on desktop
- Mobile-first responsive
